# Security Policy

We build on the side of the person, and that includes keeping the people who use our
products safe. If you find a security issue in any Kriyaetive Verse project, tell us
privately first so we can fix it before it becomes public.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Email **security@kriyaetive.com** with:
- what you found and where (repository, URL, or component),
- how to reproduce it,
- the impact you believe it has.

Please do not open a public issue, post it publicly, or test against real user data.
Give us a reasonable window to fix it before any disclosure.

## What to expect

- We acknowledge your report within 3 business days.
- We keep you updated as we investigate and ship a fix.
- We credit you when the fix ships, if you would like that.

## Scope

This policy covers Kriyaetive Verse Pvt. Ltd. projects, including the lab site, Mozhi,
digi.shopping, Writeout, and Kriyaetive Studio. Thank you for helping us protect the
people who rely on them.
